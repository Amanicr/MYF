Our Goals

We strive to make a meaningful impact in the educational and social welfare of children in Orphanages and Vulnerable Children's (OVC) Centers in Dodoma and Dar es Salaam, TANZANIA through non-paid/volunteer tutoring, social activities, fundraisers, and donations.

About Us

MYF is a youth-led organization that mobilizes young people around Tanzania. We altogether join forces and strive to make a meaningful impact in the educational and social welfare of children currently in Orphanages and Vulnerable Children's (OVC) Centers in Dodoma and Dar es Salaam, TANZANIA through non-paid/volunteer tutoring, social activities, fundraisers, and donations.
