About Us

Our Story

"Inspired by Mercy Ombeni — a compassionate friend who battled blood cancer — MYF (Mercy Youth Foundation) was founded in 2021 by students in Tanzania. Our mission is to engage in various charitable works to uplift under-resourced children. We honor our friend Mercy through our service efforts, ensuring her legacy of kindness lives on in every act of support and contribution to different centers. MYF is dedicated to making a meaningful impact on the educational and social welfare of children in Orphanages and Vulnerable Children's (OVC) Centers in Dodoma and Dar es Salaam. Our non-paid/volunteer tutoring, social activities, fundraisers, and donations are driving positive change."

About Us

MYF is a youth-led organization that mobilizes young people around Tanzania. We altogether join forces and strive to make a meaningful impact in the educational and social welfare of children currently in Orphanages and Vulnerable Children's (OVC) Centers in Dodoma and Dar es Salaam, TANZANIA through non-paid/volunteer tutoring, social activities, fundraisers, and donations.
